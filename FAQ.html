Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about JR's Football Academy? Below are some of the most common questions asked by our players and their families. If you have a question that isn't answered here, feel free to contact us!

What age groups do you offer programs for?

Our programs cater to players of all age groups, starting from ages 5 to 18. We offer specialized training for different skill levels and age brackets to ensure everyone has an appropriate learning experience.

Do I need prior football experience to join the academy?

No, prior football experience is not required to join JR's Football Academy. We welcome players of all skill levels, from beginners to advanced. Our coaching staff will assess each player's skill level and provide the appropriate training for their growth.

How can I register for a program or event?

Registration for our programs and events can be done through our website. Simply visit the 'Programs' or 'Events' page, select the program you're interested in, and click the 'Register Now' button. If you need help, don't hesitate to contact us.

What should my child bring to a training session?

For training sessions, players should bring comfortable sportswear, football boots, a water bottle, and a positive attitude! We also recommend bringing shin guards for safety during practice.

How long is each training session?

Each training session lasts for 90 minutes, focusing on skills development, drills, and match practice. Our coaches ensure every player gets the attention they need to improve and succeed.

Are there any discounts for siblings?

Yes! We offer discounts for families with multiple children enrolled in our programs. Please contact us directly for more information about sibling discounts and available offers.

What is the refund policy?

We offer refunds if a player withdraws from a program at least 14 days before the start of the session. After that, refunds will not be available. Please refer to our Terms and Conditions for full details on refunds.

How can I contact JR's Football Academy?

You can reach us through our contact page, where you'll find our phone number, email address, and a contact form. We respond to inquiries as quickly as possible.
